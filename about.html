My Story

Hello, I'm Candy

I've been drawn to the kitchen for as long as I can remember. As a child, I was always curious — watching, tasting, asking questions, and sneaking spoonfuls of batter whenever nobody was looking. Cooking wasn't just something I did; it was how I made sense of the world.

That curiosity never left me. I've always had a hunger to learn — new techniques, new flavours, new ways of combining the familiar into something unexpected. Over the years, through countless experiments and more than a few happy accidents, I slowly perfected recipes that felt truly my own: dishes and desserts that carry my flavours, my memories, and my love.

What started as cooking for family — bringing a little extra joy to the dinner table — has grown into something I never quite anticipated. Now I get to share those same recipes with all of you, and that feels like the greatest gift.

Welcome to my kitchen. I hope something here brings a little sweetness to your day.
