My Story

Hello, I'm Candy

I fell in love with baking at seven years old, perched on a stool in my grandmother's kitchen, watching her fold red bean paste into paper-thin mochi wrappers with impossible grace. She never measured anything — everything was by feel, by smell, by intuition.

That kitchen became my first classroom. I spent years recreating her recipes, then branching out into the French patisserie techniques I studied in culinary school, and eventually finding my own voice — a fusion of East and West, tradition and experimentation.

Candy's Delights began as a weekend blog where I shared my recipe notes. What I didn't expect was the community it would become — thousands of home bakers who write to tell me how Candy's mochi became a family staple, or how my cookie recipe made someone's terrible Tuesday a little sweeter.

That's why I keep baking. That's why I keep sharing.

My Journey

How It All Began

2016

The First Batch 🍪

Baked my first proper batch of chocolate chip cookies for a school bake sale. Sold out in 10 minutes and knew I was onto something.

2018

Culinary Exploration 🎓

Enrolled in a weekend patisserie course and became completely obsessed with French technique — laminated doughs, tempering chocolate, the works.

2020

The Blog is Born ✍️

Started Candy's Delights during the quiet of lockdown. What began as a personal recipe journal quickly found a devoted readership.

2022

Going Full-Time 🎉

Left my day job to pursue baking full-time. Launched my first online cooking class, sold out within 48 hours.

2024

New Chapter 🚀

Rebuilt the site, launched new recipe series, and joined forces with a small team of tasters and photographers to bring every recipe to life beautifully.

What I Believe In

My Baking Philosophy

Every recipe I create is guided by these simple truths.

🧡

Made with Intention

Every ingredient is chosen purposefully. I believe that care and attention are the most important ingredients in any recipe.

🌿

Quality Ingredients

Real butter. Fresh eggs. Good chocolate. The quality of what you put in determines everything that comes out.

📖

Recipes That Teach

I write recipes that explain the why, not just the what. Understanding technique means you can bake anything, not just follow instructions.

🤝

Community First

This site exists because of the incredible baking community. Your recreations, questions, and feedback shape every recipe I write.
